\section{Additional information}

\subsection{Source statement}
This project is a formatting demonstration derived from public metadata
and abstract-level findings of Greenhalgh et al., \emph{Cancer Cell}
2026 \supercite{Greenhalgh_2026}. It is \textbf{not} the publisher PDF, not the
article's original full text, and not an unpublished manuscript.

\subsection{Availability of materials}
All editable source files required to rebuild this demonstration project
are stored in this repository, with manuscript content maintained under
\texttt{projects/paper-sci-01/extraTex/}. Generated artifacts are
written to \texttt{.latex-cache/}, \texttt{main.pdf}, and
\texttt{main.docx} during the official build process.

\subsection{Funding}
No external funding was associated with this demonstration manuscript.
The sample exists solely to validate the \texttt{bensz-paper} package
workflow.

\subsection{Competing interests}
The authors of this demonstration template declare no competing
interests related to the sample text or build workflow.
