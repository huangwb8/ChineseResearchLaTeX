\ahnuFrontHeading{Abstract}

This public demonstration uses Nozomi Sasaki's career trajectory to examine cross-media transition across print magazines, screen production, variety formats, and creator-led platforms. The purpose is not to expose any confidential thesis text, but to verify that `thesis-ahnu-master` can render cover pages, bilingual abstracts, a table of contents, an abbreviations table, body chapters, and back matter with fully shareable sample content. The thesis organizes the case into five stages and discusses how platform choice, narrative strategy, and public response jointly shape image resilience.
