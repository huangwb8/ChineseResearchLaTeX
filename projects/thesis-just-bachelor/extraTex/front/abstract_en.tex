\justFrontHeading{Abstract}

This public demonstration studies Nozomi Sasaki's cross-media career transition and examines how content strategy, narrative pacing, and platform selection contribute to professional resilience. All materials are derived from publicly available sources rather than any confidential thesis or unpublished manuscript. The example divides the case into launch, expansion, crisis, and restart stages, then uses the analysis to verify that the undergraduate thesis template can stably render front matter, body chapters, references, and acknowledgements with shareable sample content.
