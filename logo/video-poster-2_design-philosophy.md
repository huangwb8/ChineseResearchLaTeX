## Luminous Method

This visual direction treats research writing as a disciplined energy system rather than a stack of files. The poster should feel like a control surface for scholarship: dark space, precise glow, restrained gold highlights, and a sense that every module has a reason to exist. The composition must feel meticulously crafted, calm under pressure, and unmistakably expert. It should look like the product of deep expertise rather than decorative AI spectacle.

Space should communicate hierarchy before words do. A strong central core represents the project as shared infrastructure, while surrounding modules radiate outward as focused capabilities. The layout should feel engineered, not improvised: balanced symmetry, clear lanes of movement, and generous breathing room so the eye can understand the system in one glance. Every distance, edge, and alignment should suggest painstaking attention and master-level execution.

Color should stay within a narrow, intentional range. Use deep academic navy as the main field, electric cyan for intelligence and live tooling, and controlled metallic gold as the mark of polish, standards, and publication-grade output. The glow must be crisp rather than hazy, with luminous edges that feel like illuminated instruments in a dark lab. Nothing should feel muddy, noisy, or casually blended.

Form language should merge scientific instrumentation with document architecture. Think modular panels, precise circuit traces, document silhouettes, node clusters, subtle bracket-like geometry, and abstract references to LaTeX, workflows, and publication pipelines. These forms should imply AI-assisted rigor, platform thinking, and end-to-end research support without relying on long explanations. The image must reward close viewing with small crafted details while remaining instantly legible at a distance.

Typography is not the star of the generated base image. The generated canvas should reserve elegant text-safe zones and visual anchors, while final readable text can be added with deterministic local typesetting. This keeps the poster pristine and professional. The final result should feel painstakingly refined, as if every glow, border, icon, and margin has been labored over by someone at the very top of their field.
